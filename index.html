About

Profile & Recruiting

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Digital Media at Seoul Women's University. Before joining Seoul Women's University, I worked at Samsung Electronics, where I conducted research on AI accelerators, RISC-V CPU design, and RISC-V vector engines. I received my Ph.D. in Engineering from Korea University under the supervision of Prof. Taeweon Suh.

I am looking for prospective PhD and MSc students interested in computer architecture and AI systems research. Interested students are welcome to contact me at psw0113@swu.ac.kr.
